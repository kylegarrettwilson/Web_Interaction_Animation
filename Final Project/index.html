Welcome to Smile Black, an audio-visual project by a self proclaimed audiophile. My name is Kyle Wilson and I have loved music my entire life. My musical journey began at a young age with the piano and progressed through the entire band classroom until I landed on percussion, the drum kit to be more exact. Drumming has been my passion for nearly twenty years and I strive to progress in this art every day. About a year ago I moved to Portland, Oregon to try and make drumming my career. Just like they always say, the life of a working musician is not glamorous. After a couple gigs with a few bands, I decided to create this YouTube project in hopes of showing the world what my sound is. I hope you enjoy and subscribe if you do. Kyle.
